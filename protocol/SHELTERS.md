# Shelters

A **Shelter** is a role that a Storage Node can take on.

Shelters are a predictable place where data should be sent when communicating with a specific individual.

## Core Idea

- Storage Nodes can become Shelters.
- A Shelter is tied to one or many identities.
- Senders send data to all of individual's Shelter's to ensure predictable delivery.

## Access Models

Shelters may be offered in two ways:

- **Free**: anyone can use the Shelter without payment.
- **Paid (FairShares)**: access requires an active monthly FairShares payment.

## Why Shelters Matter
Shelters provide a stable destination for person-to-person communication, so clients know where to send data for a given individual, even when he is offline.
